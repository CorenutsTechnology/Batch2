The City of Pearls

Hyderabad is known as the City of Pearls, as it had once flourished as a global center for trade of rare diamonds, emeralds as
well as natural pearls. Mecca Masjid, Charminar, Golconda fort, QutubShahi tombs, Paigah tombs are some of the historic structures.
